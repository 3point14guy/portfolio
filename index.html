About Me

I’ve always had the drive to follow my passions which has made me fortunate enough to do many things with my life, from working at public aquariums diving with sharks, to teaching English in a high school in Thailand, to helping streamline processes for the nation’s largest wireless carrier.

Through experiences like these, there has emerged a unifying theme of solving problems to help others in two major ways; achieving their goals and being more productive. I came to a point where I realized I needed to level-up my skill set in order to continue helping others in a meaningful way.

I am eager to take my skills to the next level by upgrading from my standard problem solving toolbox into a great big workshop powered by coding. My goal is to make myself a vital asset to work teams by improving process and creating novel solutions through coding.
